About

Understanding and building things.

I'm Tinashe, a mechanical engineering student who genuinely enjoys the messy middle of building things: the CAD that has error messages and doesn't print, the prototype that almost works, the simulation that doesn't match the bench test.

My work sits at the intersection of mechanics, electronics, and a bit of code. I've spent time trying to understand where the problem lies and how to fix it, and I'm happiest when there's a deadline and a soldering iron nearby.

Outside, I go hiking, fix faulty home appliances, and read books. Currenly reading "The Odyssey" by Homer (in translation). I also have a soft spot for cooking shows and documentaries about engineering feats.

GutterBot — Autonomous Gutter Cleaning Robot

2025 Group Project Multi-Disciplinary Team
Product Design Robotics CAD Modeling Systems Engineering Hardware Development Circuit Design Production Planning Cost Analysis

Overview

GutterBot is an autonomous rain gutter cleaning robot designed for aging homeowners and property managers across the EU. The product eliminates the hazards and inefficiencies of manual gutter maintenance by providing a remote-controlled, waterless debris removal solution. This multi-disciplinary project integrated mechanical engineering, electrical systems, software control, and production planning across a team of four engineering students.

Problem & Solution

Traditional gutter cleaning requires dangerous ladder work, heavy manual labor, and costly professional services. GutterBot replaces this with an autonomous system that removes leaves, twigs, and debris using adjustable brushes and precise sensors—all controlled remotely from the ground. The compact, lightweight design minimizes user risk while reducing long-term maintenance costs.

Key Design Features

Debris Removal Rotating augers and adjustable brushes

Engineering Analysis

Drive Calculations: Motor sizing determined from force analysis accounting for gutter inclines, debris resistance, and track friction. Driving mechanism verified for continuous operation across typical residential gutter slopes.

Debris Removal System: Auger speeds calculated to optimize material collection without jamming. Brush tension and rotation rate tuned for effective debris extraction while minimizing power draw.

Power Management: Battery capacity and motor specifications selected to provide 45+ minutes continuous runtime. Energy calculations verified against dust-proof motor controller specifications and charging times.

FMEA Analysis: Design Failure Mode and Effects Analysis identified 29 potential failure modes with mitigation strategies for critical components including motor failure, track slippage, and sensor malfunction.

Development Process

Mechanical Design: Complete 3D CAD model developed in SolidWorks showing all assemblies, mechanisms, and component integration. Design optimized for manufacturability and user assembly.

Electrical Architecture: DC motor control circuit designed with Bluetooth transceiver for wireless operation. Push-button interface provides intuitive user control with safety interlocks.

Manufacturing Planning: Production workflow designed for 10,000 unit annual volume. Assembly sequence optimized with detailed process times, workforce requirements, and OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) calculations.

Cost Analysis: Bill of Materials compiled with 40+ components. Total production cost calculated at €42.50 per unit with target selling price of €229. Manufacturing cost breakdown includes material, labor, overhead, and assembly.

Project Scope

This comprehensive product design project spanned three milestones over 10 weeks:

  • Milestone 1: Concept development, market analysis, requirements definition, functional structure, drive concept, and preliminary FMEA
  • Milestone 2: CAD modeling, motor/battery selection, human-machine interface design, technology selection, and requirements documentation
  • Milestone 3: Technical drawings, circuit diagrams, control software flowcharts, bill of materials, production planning, and cost calculations

Target Market & Business Case

Primary target: EU homeowners age 55+ and property managers. Market positioning emphasizes safety (eliminating ladder risk), cost savings (one-time investment vs. recurring professional services), and sustainability (waterless operation). EU market estimated at 28 million owner-occupied homes with significant older homeowner demographic.

Conclusion

GutterBot demonstrates a complete product development cycle from concept through production-ready documentation. The design successfully balances safety, functionality, cost-effectiveness, and manufacturability. Comprehensive engineering analysis, risk mitigation through FMEA, and detailed production planning establish a viable pathway to market for this innovative home maintenance solution.
